Agglomerative Information Bottleneck (AIB). We test AIB on the problem of finding an discriminatively optimal quantization of a mixure of Gaussian. Left: mixture of three Gaussians. The 2-D values are uniformly quantized in 20x20 bins. Other figures: AIB clusterings. The grayed areas correspond to feature values (bins) that did not collect any evidence and so remains unassigned. AIB then merges bins by preserving as much a spossible the information about the corresponding Gaussian blob identity.
